OUR STORY

About ElevateLend —
Elevating Australian Business

We exist to give every Australian business owner access to the best funding options, without the complexity, the jargon, or the runaround.

Our Mission

To give every Australian business owner access to the best funding options — without the complexity.

We believe business owners should spend their time building their businesses, not navigating the labyrinth of Australian lending. We built ElevateLend to be the bridge between great businesses and the capital they need to grow.

Our Story

Born from frustration. Built with purpose.

ElevateLend was founded by finance professionals who spent years watching Australian businesses struggle to access funding. The problem wasn’t a lack of lenders — it was that business owners had no way to efficiently compare their options.

The traditional process was broken. Walk into a bank, fill out a 30-page application, wait 3–6 weeks, and hope for the best. Get declined? Start the whole process again at the next bank. Meanwhile, the business opportunity you needed the capital for has already passed.

We saw the rise of alternative lenders — fintechs, specialist financiers, private credit funds — creating more options than ever before. But with more options came more complexity. How does a busy business owner know which of the 200+ Australian business lenders is right for them?

That’s where ElevateLend comes in. We built a platform that takes the complexity out of business lending. One simple application. One dedicated specialist. 75+ lenders compared. The best rate, matched to your business, in minutes — not weeks.

Today, we’ve helped over 1,200 Australian businesses access more than $2.4 billion in funding. And we’re just getting started.

Our Values

What we stand for

Four principles guide every decision we make and every interaction we have.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no surprises, no fine print that changes the deal. We show you exactly what each lender offers — the rates, the fees, the terms — so you can make an informed decision.

Speed

Business doesn’t wait, and neither should your funding. We’ve built our entire process around getting you from application to funded as fast as humanly possible. Most businesses see funds within 24–48 hours.

Expertise

Our team knows the lending landscape inside out. We know which lenders have appetite for which industries, which are flexible on credit, and which offer the best terms for your specific situation.

Integrity

We recommend the best option for your business, not the one that pays us the highest commission. If we don’t think a loan is right for you, we’ll tell you. Your success is our success.

Compliance & Regulation

Licensed, regulated, and compliant

We operate under Australian financial services regulations and take our compliance obligations seriously.

Australian Credit Licence
ElevateLend holds an Australian Credit Licence (ACL), the regulatory requirement for providing credit assistance in Australia.
Responsible lending
We comply with responsible lending obligations, ensuring you’re only connected with products that are suitable for your circumstances.
Data protection
Your data is protected with bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption. We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles and never sell your data to third parties.
Dispute resolution
We are a member of the Australian Financial Complaints Authority (AFCA), providing you with access to free and independent dispute resolution.
Industry membership
We are active members of relevant industry bodies and committed to upholding the highest standards of professional conduct in Australian lending.
