Eligibility

Is private lending right for you?

Private lending is best suited for borrowers with clear transactions, available security, and a defined exit strategy.

Clear exit strategy
How will the loan be repaid? Sale of property, refinance to a bank, revenue from the business — we need to see a realistic path to repayment.
Security available
Most private lending is secured against real property (residential or commercial), although some lenders will consider other forms of security.
Minimum $500K requirement
Private lending facilities start at $500K. For smaller amounts, our standard business lending options may be a better fit.
Experienced borrowers preferred
While not always required, experience in the relevant asset class or industry strengthens your deal and broadens lender appetite.
