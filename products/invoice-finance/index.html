HOW IT WORKS

Turn your invoices into cash in 3 steps

Issue an invoice, upload it, and get up to 95% of the value deposited the same day.

1

Issue your invoice

Invoice your customer as normal. Upload the invoice to your facility (or your provider integrates directly with your accounting software).

2

Receive up to 95%

The financier advances up to 95% of the invoice value into your bank account — typically within 24 hours.

3

Customer pays, balance released

When your customer pays the invoice, the remaining balance (minus fees) is released to you. The cycle repeats with every new invoice.

KEY FEATURES

Why invoice finance through ElevateLend?

Your invoices are already assets — invoice finance turns them into immediate working capital.

Grows with your revenue

Your facility scales automatically as your invoicing grows. More invoices means more available funding — no need to renegotiate.

Same-day funding

Most providers release funds within 24 hours of invoice submission. Some offer same-day settlement for approved invoices.

No property security

Your invoices are the security. No real estate, no personal guarantees (with some providers), no additional assets required.

Choose your invoices

Finance all invoices (whole-ledger) or select specific invoices as needed (spot factoring). You control which invoices to fund.

Confidential option

Choose disclosed (your customer knows) or confidential (they do not). Many businesses prefer confidential to maintain customer relationships.

Improve your balance sheet

Reduce debtors, improve cash conversion and strengthen your balance sheet metrics — important for tenders and contracts.

WHO IT’S FOR

Wholesalers & distributors

Businesses supplying goods to retailers and other businesses on trade terms.

Fast-growth companies

Businesses growing faster than their cash flow can support — invoice finance bridges the gap without equity dilution.

Government contractors

Businesses with government contracts often face extended payment terms — invoice finance solves this.
