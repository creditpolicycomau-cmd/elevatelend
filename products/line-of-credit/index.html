HOW IT WORKS

How a line of credit works

Think of it as a business overdraft — an approved credit limit you can draw from whenever you need, then repay and redraw.

1

Get approved

Apply in 2 minutes. We match you with lenders offering the best line of credit for your business.

2

Draw what you need

Access funds instantly up to your approved limit. Draw $5K or $50K — whatever your business needs right now.

3

Repay & redraw

Pay interest only on drawn funds. As you repay, your available credit replenishes — ready whenever you need it again.

4

Ongoing access

Your facility stays open and available. No need to reapply each time you need funds — it is always there.

KEY FEATURES

Built for businesses that need flexibility

A line of credit gives you the control to access capital on your terms, without the rigidity of a term loan.

Only pay for what you use

Interest is charged only on the amount you have drawn, not your total limit. Undrawn funds cost you nothing.

Revolving access

As you repay, your credit limit replenishes automatically. No need to reapply — funds are always available.

Instant drawdowns

Once approved, draw funds instantly via bank transfer. Most lenders process drawdowns same day or within 24 hours.

No property security

Most line of credit products are unsecured. No property or asset security required — approval based on cash flow.

Flexible repayment

Pay back on your schedule — weekly, fortnightly or monthly. Many products have no minimum repayment amounts.

Dedicated support

Your personal funding specialist helps you find the right facility and supports you through setup and beyond.

WHO IT’S FOR
